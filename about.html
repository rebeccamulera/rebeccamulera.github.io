This is My Story

From childhood to adulthood I created multimedia designs in my grandmother's in-home art studio. This is where I learned the basics of design as I created art through the use of sketching, painting and sewing. My grandmother would take me to workshops at the local art museum or to art classes at a professional studio. My enthusiasm for design grew as I improved as an artist. 

I have utilized my artistic capabilities in other areas of my life as well. My exposure to professional photography started when I was a child, watching and learning from my mother as she grew her photography business from scratch. My mother's drive and ambition is what motivated and inspired me to pursue a career in doing what I love, Interior Design.
